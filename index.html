EVERYTHING IN THE WORLD BEGAN

2023, VIDEO INSTALLATION, 7 MINUTES (LOOPED)

You must sit down, says Love, and taste my meat:
So I did sit and eat.

EVERYTHING IN THE WORLD BEGAN is a dance-charged video poem that explores what it means to be in relation with others in order to understand what it means to be made of flesh and to be vulnerable.
